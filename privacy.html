Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-06

This Privacy Policy explains how Yada (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use the Yada mobile application (the “App”).

Summary

  • No account required. We do not require you to create an account to use the App.
  • Minimal data. The App primarily stores your preferences on your device (e.g., theme, Pro entitlement flag).
  • No sale of personal data. We do not sell personal information.
  • Notifications are optional. If you enable reminders, your device schedules them locally.

If you have questions, contact: privacy@YOURDOMAIN.com

Information We Collect

1) Information you provide

The App does not require you to submit personal information (such as name, phone number, address, or account credentials).

2) Information stored on your device

Yada stores certain settings locally on your device to make the App work smoothly. Examples include:

  • Theme preference (e.g., light, dark, system)
  • Pro entitlement status flag (e.g., proEntitlement)
  • Other app settings you choose to enable

This data is stored locally (for example, using platform storage such as preferences). It is not designed to identify you.

3) Notifications

If you opt in to reminders/notifications, the App requests permission from your operating system. Notifications are scheduled on your device and can be disabled anytime in your device settings.

4) Analytics / tracking

By default, Yada does not use third-party analytics SDKs or tracking technology. If this changes, we will update this policy before enabling such services.

How We Use Information

We use information only to:

  • Operate and improve core App functionality (e.g., remember your theme setting)
  • Enable Pro features if you purchased access
  • Deliver optional reminders if you enable notifications
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited cases:

  • Legal requirements (e.g., to comply with applicable law or lawful requests)
  • Service providers only if needed to run the App (for example, App Store / Play Store purchase processing is handled by Apple/Google)

In-App Purchases

If you purchase Pro access, the transaction is processed by Apple (App Store) and/or Google (Play). We do not receive your full payment details. We may store a local flag to reflect entitlement status on your device.

Data Retention

Settings stored locally remain on your device until you remove the App or clear the App’s data/storage. We do not operate a user database for Yada at this time.

Your Choices

Notifications

You can enable/disable notifications anytime via your device settings.

Local app data

You can remove the App to delete locally stored settings.

Children’s Privacy

Yada is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us and we will address it.

International Users

If you use the App outside your home country, your device may process data under local laws. Because Yada primarily stores settings locally, cross-border transfer is generally not applicable unless we add online services in the future.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” above and publish the new version on this page.
